PLAYER INSTALL
--------------
1. Close KSP.
2. Copy GameData/KoobalSearchEngine/ into your KSP GameData/ folder (merge/overwrite).
3. Ensure Harmony 2 (000_Harmony) is installed — see README.txt in the mod folder.
4. Launch KSP; KSP.log should show: [Koobal] Koobal Search Engine v0.8.5.1 active.
